METHODOLOGY
How it works
Career averages from ufcstats.com, fight results from 2015 to December 2025, style membership scores from ML clustering. 19 features total.
01
Feature engineering
Net strike edge (slpm × accuracy − sapm × absorption), grapple dominance, style clash score, fighter style membership (striker/wrestler probability), KO/submission rates, weight class.
02
XGBoost + Optuna
50-trial Bayesian tuning. Each fight appears twice — once per fighter as F1 — balanced 50/50 dataset. Only fights where both fighters have full stats included.
03
Why 70% is hard
Papers claiming 70%+ either use in-fight stats (leakage), betting odds as a feature, or small handpicked test sets. With career averages only, 64% is close to the statistical ceiling — a 2019 paper by Bunker & Thabtah hit the same number.
04
JS surrogate
Logistic regression fit on XGBoost's own predictions achieves 90.5% agreement. Predictions run in the browser with no backend round-trip.
